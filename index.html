Wine & Deisgn

Creativity, Growth Mindset & Trying New Things

My Wine and Design Instagram account began one summer when I decided I needed to push myself creatively and improve my design skills. I made a goal that each day for the rest of my time in Connecticut, where I was completing an internship, I would make something, anything, in Sketch and send it into the world via a new Instagram account, just for my creative endeavors. Since that summer I’ve posted many more projects in a variety of mediums as I’ve learned, experimented and played. Even though I don’t post daily, the account is fun to look back on and I love having a place to collect my creative tidbits.
